IoT Smart Farming

Revolutionizing Agriculture with IoT

  • → IoT Sensors Deployment: Low-cost IoT sensors monitor key environmental factors like soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and pH levels in real-time.
⬇
  • → Data Collection & Analysis: These sensors send data to the cloud, where AI algorithms process it to provide insights on optimal planting schedules, irrigation needs, and pest control strategies.
⬇
  • → Precision Irrigation: The system triggers irrigation systems based on real-time soil moisture levels, preventing water wastage and ensuring crops receive the right amount of water.
⬇
  • → Crop Health Monitoring: AI analyzes sensor data along with satellite imagery (or drone data) to detect early signs of diseases or nutrient deficiencies in crops. Alerts are sent to farmers with recommendations for intervention (e.g., targeted pesticide application or nutrient supplementation).
